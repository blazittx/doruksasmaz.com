Stick Fighter 3D
3D 2 player boxing game featuring stickmans and tons of sparkles.

Quick Summary

My Role: Game Designer & Developer (Freelance)

Development Time: 4 months (Aug 2021 - Nov 2021)

Team Size: Worked Solo

Developed by: HiHoy

Published by: HiHoy

Platform(s): Web & Android

Short Description: Stick Fighter has 3D physics-based combat simulation. While playing this fighting game with 1 player and 2 player modes, you will have a great time alone or with your friends. Using the power of your fist and kick, you have to make the right moves at the right time. Pay attention to your energy bar. Make your best move to your opponent by making your special hit as soon as the energy bar reaches its maximum level. Protect yourself from your opponent's special hit by collapsing or jumping.

What I Did?

Developed a modular, expandable, designer-friendly, and ready-to-use fighting game blueprint in Unity.

The blueprint was used as a template for Stick Fighter 3D, a free-to-play fighting game that reached millions of players across web and mobile platforms.

Analyzed various games and deconstructed their designs to develop a designer mentality.
