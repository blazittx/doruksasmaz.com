Beer & Plunder
3D co-op management game with fighting mechanics.

Quick Summary

My Role: Game Designer (Intern)

Contributed for : 1 month (Jan 2023 - Feb 2023)

Team Size: 6 (2 Developers, 2 Artists, 2 Designers)

Developed by: Oddiko (still in development)

Published by: TBA

Platform(s): TBA

Short Description: Beer & Plunder is a co-op management game with fighting mechanics where you and your friends get to take on the roles of viking bartenders and run your own viking pub! As Pub-owners, you must work together in a hectic mix of serving and fighting in order to fulfill both the reasonable, and unreasonable requests of your hot-headed customers.

What I Did?

Contributed to the development of award winning game Beer & Plunder, working with a team of 6 using Agile practices.

Focused on enhancing the game's UI/UX and implementing quality of life improvements, while also providing documented design ideas to the team.

Transformed future game concepts into practical prototypes to aid the teams development process.
