Agent In Depth
daring 2D action-platformer featuring fast-paced action and instant-death combat.

Quick Summary

My Roles: Game Designer, Developer, Project Manager (Founder, Self-Employed)

Development Time: 8 months (May 2022 - Jan 2023)

Team Size: 4 (2 Developers, 1 Artist, 1 Designer)

Developed by: Naturepixel Studios

Published by: Next in Game

Platform(s): Steam & Itch.io (Windows, Linux, macOS)

Short Description: Agent in Depth is a daring action-platformer featuring fast-paced action and instant-death combat. You’ll run, jump, aim, and deflect bullets with your katana to blow up your enemies. No checkpoints, no upgrades: just pure skills. Deflect, die, learn, repeat. Be better, be faster.
