Ape Ascent
Unleash your primal fury in a vertigo-inducing VR climb for dominance.

Platform(s): Itch.io (Windows, Linux)

Short Description:
Embrace your primal instincts in Ape Ascent, a thrilling VR adventure that catapults you into the untamed world of mountain-dwelling apes. As the alpha of your group, your journey to the summit is anything but a peaceful climb. Engage in heart-pounding battles against rival apes, using an arsenal of unique weapons to assert your dominance.

What I Did?

As the 3D artist and developer for Ape Ascent, I was instrumental in constructing the rich, immersive environment that forms the game's core. I developed intricate 3D models, from the rugged terrain of the mountain to the uniquely animated ape characters, each embodying a lifelike and visceral physicality. I also implemented VR mechanics, ensuring smooth, responsive gameplay that allowed players to engage with the world intuitively. My work sculpted an evocative, tangible virtual reality, providing players with a truly immersive and dynamic gaming experience.
