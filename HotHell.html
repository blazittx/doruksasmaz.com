Short Description:
Step into the heart of darkness with Hot Hell, a high-intensity 3D shooter set within the ever-changing halls of a demonic hotel. In this game, your hands, deformed and imbued with deadly power, are your only defense against an onslaught of hellish adversaries. With the environment procedurally generated, no two experiences are the same.

What I Did?

In the development of Hot Hell, I served as the 3D artist and developer, creating a hauntingly atmospheric and endlessly evolving hotel environment. With every level procedurally generated, I designed a myriad of eerie settings, infusing each with distinct elements of dread and suspense. The demonic adversaries, meticulously detailed and animated, were designed to instill fear and challenge in players.
